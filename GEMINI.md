# PROJECT CONTEXT: COS Veterinary Clinic Website

## 1. Brand Identity & Voice
**Business Name:** COS Veterinary Clinic  
**Tagline:** "Sincere Care. Transparent Pricing. Officially Protected."  
**Mission:** To provide accessible, high-quality veterinary care with a focus on transparency (no surprise bills) and genuine affection for animals ("We treat them like family").  
**Tone:** Professional, Approachable, Reassuring, Transparent, and Modern.

**Visual Identity:**
- **Primary Color:** Medical Teal / Cyan (Reference: Clinic cabinetry and logo).
- **Secondary Colors:** Clean White, Slate Gray (Text).
- **Aesthetic:** "Glassmorphism," Clean Medical UI, Modern Web (Magic UI/React Bits style).

## 2. Key Personnel
- **Head Veterinarian:** Dr. Cherry (Known for her "sincere and caring attitude" and "comprehensive explanations").
- **Staff:** Sir Paul, Sir Renz.
- **Reputation:** The team is frequently described as "Animal Lovers" who are "approachable" and "resilient."

## 3. Unique Feature: "The Graduates" (Officially Protected)
- **Concept:** A digital "Wall of Fame" for pets who have completed their full vaccination courses.
- **Terminology:** Pets are referred to as "Graduates" or "Officially Protected."
- **Visuals:** Certificate-style presentation (Class of 2026).
- **Goal:** To celebrate responsible pet ownership and build a community feeling.

## 4. Services Offered
- **Wellness:** Routine Check-ups, Deworming, Vaccinations (The "Road to Graduation").
- **Medical:** Sick Consultations, Confinement, Surgery.
- **Amenities:** Parking space available, Air-conditioned waiting area.

## 7. User Journeys (UX)
- **The Worried Owner:** Lands on site -> Sees "Transparent Pricing" and "Caring Attitude" -> Books appointment via Wizard.
